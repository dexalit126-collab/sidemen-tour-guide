Authentic Local Lunch at Juni’s House

A Cultural Dining Experience in Sidemen, Bali

← Back to Tours

Experience Traditional Balinese Home Cooking

Join us for an authentic local lunch at Juni’s family home in Sidemen village. Enjoy fresh Balinese dishes cooked with local ingredients, while learning about village life, family traditions, and Balinese culture.

This is more than a meal — it’s a cultural dining experience where you connect with a local family, hear meaningful stories, and taste authentic home-cooked recipes passed down through generations.

Price

Starting from IDR 250K (~$18) per person

Minimum 2 participants

Inclusions

  • Drink: Welcome traditional Balinese drink
  • Appetiser: Fresh local starters
  • Main Course: Authentic Balinese home-cooked dishes
  • Dessert: Local sweet treats

    • Experience Highlights

  • Cultural storytelling & village traditions
  • Interaction with local family & learning about daily life
  • Hands-on chance to try traditional Balinese recipes

    • Transport from the Sidemen area can be arranged upon request.

    Staying Outside Sidemen?
    Private transport can be arranged for an additional fee depending on location.
